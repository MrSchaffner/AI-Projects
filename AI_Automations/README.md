# AI Automations

Automations Created Using AI Tools

### Weather Based Staffing Tool

Miami Fixture Bayside Brews & Scoops had everything - the ideal location, a rockin’ menu, and professional staff. But Weather and staffing kept getting in the way. Once they realized they were related, they were halfway to a solution. 

The Manager, Emily Davis, came to us for help. By Leveraging Zapier’s AI Automation Tools, we removed the guesswork and helped her with weather, staffing and marketing insights. These were sent daily, before opening time and utilized real weather data.
